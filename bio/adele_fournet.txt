
Adele Fournet is a composer, music producer and video artist.  She composes for acoustic instruments, works with synthesizers and electronic music, and writes and records in multiple pop and rock genres. This versatile musical background leads to a rich array of cinematic soundscapes and imaginative videos.  She holds a BA in Music from the New College of Florida, an MA in Music from New York University, and is currently pursuing a PhD in Ethnomusicology at NYU.  Primary collaborations include The Soon-Another, E.lind Dance, and Sebastian Apolinario
